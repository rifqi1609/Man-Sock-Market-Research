## ⚠️ Problem Background
**PT Adidaya Fesyen Indonesia** is in the process of developing a new product line. To ensure a successful launch, we have initiated research into the **Man Socks Market** on Tokopedia to:
*   **Sharpen Pricing Strategy:** Understand the competitive price landscape to position our product effectively.
*   **Leverage Organic Reviews:** Analyze the impact of organic customer feedback on boosting sales.
*   **Reduce Ad Dependency:** Build a sustainable sales model that minimizes reliance on high advertising budgets through organic growth.

## 🎯 Project Objective
The ultimate goal is to attain a **0.01% market share** for Man Socks on Tokopedia within **one year**. This will be achieved by defining a robust pricing strategy and utilizing organic reviews to drive consistent organic sales.

## 📊 Data Overview & Methodology

### Data Collection
*   **Source:** Tokopedia (Sub-category: Man Sock).
*   **Sample Size:** 327 listing products across Indonesia.
*   **Extraction Date:** December 1, 2025.
*   **Condition:** Raw data collected without special treatment during scraping.

### Analysis Method
*   **Descriptive Statistics:** Used to interpret data distributions and summarize market trends.
*   **Inferential Statistics:** Applied to gain deeper insights and support strategic conclusions.
